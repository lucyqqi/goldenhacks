eduscribe.ai

A restorative language learning model used to create access to understanding textual and auditory documents, whenever and wherever.


Demo

Our mission is to break barriers and ensure equitable and intersectional access to all information within different communities.

With the instant translation feature, you can learn and improve your comprehension of any text or audio document.

Our audio transcription summarizes text from speech for improved accessibility which can be combined with the translation feature.

Eduscribe.ai is compatible with both textual and auditory transcripts. EDAI transcribes audio into text or takes existing text, analyzes it then translates it into a target language.

Additional features include summarization and sentient analysis. Using these functions will make understanding your classes, documents, or anything else easier.
